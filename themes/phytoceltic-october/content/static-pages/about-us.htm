Bioactive Molecules for Tomorrow’s Health


Phytoceltic Life Sciences UG (Haftungsbeschränkt) is German’s privately-owned company in the field of phyto-pharmaceuticals and plant biotechnology industries.

Phytoceltic’s strategy is the current state-of-the-art R&D in eco-efficient breeding technologies and novel biopharmaceuticals developing and production. These biopharmaceuticals will be scientifically validated and harmonized by standards of good agricultural practice (GAP), good manufacturing practice (GMP), good laboratory practice (GLP), the European Medicines Evaluation Agency (EMEA) guidelines. This includes management information system (PMIS) for both technical data on quality and production.

The company’s development plans are mostly focused on further international market expansion through the export, contract manufacturing in foreign markets and marketing activities. 

Phytoceltic goes beyond industry standards and qualities to ensure patients receive clean, safe, and pure phyto-pharmaceutical products.

The company will provide a novel training approach to improve undergraduate researchers at the interface of plant biotechnology, system biotransformation and metabolic profiling.

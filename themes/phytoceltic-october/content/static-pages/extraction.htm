We are interested in investigating the applicability of supercritical fluid extraction (SFE-CO2) as a useful industrial technique for extraction phytopharmaceuticals. The green solvents “CO2; liquid-like behavior” is not toxic, non-flammable, high-purity solvent, recyclable and leaves no residual contaminant. Pressure, temperature and flow rate are three keys parameters affecting the extraction rate of different ingredients in one process. Analytical techniques as chromatography (GLC, TLC, HPLC) and mass spectrometry (GC-MS, LC-MS) will be used after extraction

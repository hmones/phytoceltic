Company Code of Conduct


Phytoceltic LS is defined by the principles our employees embrace, together with the Corporate Governance Index, form the framework of values guiding the operations of our company. This refers to our vison for sustainable development and based on the UN global compact's ten principles, the universal declaration of environment and human rights responsibilities. The code applies to Phytoceltic Life Sciences and all associated business partners and are subjecting to regular review.

Company Values


Phytoceltic LS’ mission is rooted in unlocking the potential of bioactive molecules and therapeutic potential of medicinal plants. We are guided by several fundamental principles. We strive to be productive at all times in accordance with ecological and ethical principles:

Future – towards sustainable development in Germany.

Equality – we are here to change lives.

Empowering Women –for development and balanced society.

Safety Policy – Safety and health of the workplace for all employees.

Innovative – we keep challenging ourselves with new targets to explore and develop new medicines targeting numerous disorders.

Excellence – we strive to operate according to the highest standards.

Work Ethics – We value punctuality, regular attendance, self-respect, self-discipline, team spirit, cooperation skills, eagerness to learn new, respect colleagues and avoid discriminating against and company rules.

Sustainability – We remain dedicated to a long-term, value-oriented business strategy, based on considerate use of nature's treasures.
